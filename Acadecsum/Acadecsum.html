Academic Summary

I am currently working on my dissertation, Liquid Borders: Latina/o Media Production in Chicago. My work utilises a comparative media studies approach that engages ethnic, Latina/o, hip-hop, and cultural studies. My research interests include:

  • * Media Activism
  • * The Transitions of New Media
  • * The Production of Cultural Networks
  • * Representations of Race and Ethnicity
  • * Critical Geography
  • * Borderlands Studies of Media

I have presented at the Society for Cinema and Media Studies on the Chicago Latino Film Festival and Univision Television. I received a BS from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Psychology and Cinema Studies (2007) and an MA in Communication and Culture from Indiana University in Bloomington (2009).
